SaaS ARCHITECTURE

B.E.A.T. PASIG
COMMUNITY INTELLIGENCE

The Broadcast of Events, Alerts, & Traffic (B.E.A.T.) is a centralized software-as-a-service platform engineered to bridge the gap between Pasig City residents and emergency responders. By utilizing high-integrity data logistics and a distributed reporting framework, the system transforms community vigilance into actionable, real-time intelligence for city-wide administration.

MISSION

To build a secure, high-integrity reporting pipeline that ensures community concerns reach the Pasig Command Center with zero latency.

VISION

To set the standard for smart-city safety initiatives, leveraging advanced engineering to cultivate a safer, more connected urban environment.

SYSTEM CAPABILITIES

CORE FEATURES

Incident Reporting

Encrypted submission portal for reporting public hazards and security concerns directly to authorities.

Geo-Tagged Alerts

Precise location-based broadcasting that ensures residents receive time-sensitive alerts for their specific zone.

Live Status Feed

A centralized dashboard monitoring city metrics, weather conditions, and community patrol updates.

Secure Data Logs

High-integrity relational database management ensuring all community reports are securely archived and trackable.

Mobile Optimization

Fully responsive interface designed for rapid reporting on-the-go across all mobile and tablet devices.

24/7 Availability

Automated system uptime monitoring to ensure the command interface remains operational at all times.
